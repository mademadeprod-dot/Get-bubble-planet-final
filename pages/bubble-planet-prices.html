Tips

How to get the best price

📅
Book weekday slots
Monday–Thursday sessions are typically cheaper than weekends. Can save you £5–10 per ticket.
🎟
Check for discount codes
Promotional codes are released periodically. Check our discount page before you book.
👶
Kids under 2 go free
In most cities, children under 2 enter free with a paying adult. No separate ticket needed.
⏰
Book early for peak dates
School holidays and bank holidays sell out fastest. Book 1–2 weeks ahead for busy periods.
