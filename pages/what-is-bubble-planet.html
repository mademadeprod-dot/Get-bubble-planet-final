What is Bubble Planet?

Everything you need to know — what it is, what to expect, who it's for and where to find it.

What is Bubble Planet?

Bubble Planet is a large-scale immersive experience designed to transport visitors into a colourful, surreal world of giant bubbles, luminous installations and interactive environments. Produced by Exhibition Hub in partnership with Fever, it has rapidly become one of the most talked-about experiences globally — with locations across the UK, US, Australia, Canada and Europe.

Unlike a traditional exhibition, Bubble Planet is a walk-through experience where each room tells a different visual story. There are no rides, no fixed route — you explore at your own pace.

How long does it last?

Most visitors spend 60–90 minutes inside. There's no set duration — you move at your own pace within your booked time slot.

Who is it for?

Bubble Planet is designed for all ages. Most popular with families with children (3–14), couples, friend groups and photography enthusiasts. Children under 2 typically enter free.

Where is it?

Currently operating in 13 cities: London, Manchester, New York, Washington DC, Atlanta, Bellevue/Seattle, Phoenix/Tempe, Dallas, Sydney, Melbourne, Montréal, Toronto and Berlin.

How to book

Tickets are booked online through Fever. Select your city, choose a date and time, pay securely — mobile tickets arrive by email. Advance booking is strongly recommended as sessions sell out, especially on weekends.
